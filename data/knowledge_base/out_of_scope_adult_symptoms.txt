TOPIC: Out of Scope - Adult Symptoms and Non-Health Queries
SOURCE: WHO/NHS/CDC (public guidelines)
LANGUAGE: en

This document defines the boundaries of the pediatric triage assistant.

SCOPE DEFINITION:
This assistant is designed exclusively for pediatric (child) health triage. It is intended to help parents and caregivers assess symptoms in children aged 0-18 years.

OUT OF SCOPE QUERIES:

Adult health symptoms:
- Symptoms described as belonging to an adult (husband, wife, mother, father, self)
- Examples: "My husband has chest pain", "I have a headache", "My wife has fever"
- These require adult medical resources, not pediatric triage

Non-health queries:
- Product recommendations not related to health (strollers, car seats, toys)
- Shipping or delivery questions
- Account or order management
- Recipe or nutrition questions unrelated to illness
- General parenting advice not related to symptoms
- School or developmental questions

Mental health crises:
- Suicidal thoughts or self-harm
- Severe psychiatric symptoms
- These require specialized mental health services

Veterinary queries:
- Symptoms in pets or animals

Dental emergencies:
- Toothache, dental injuries (refer to dentist)

Chronic disease management:
- Long-term management of diagnosed conditions (diabetes, epilepsy, etc.)
- Medication adjustments for chronic conditions
- These require ongoing care from a specialist

HOW TO RESPOND TO OUT OF SCOPE QUERIES:
- Acknowledge the query politely
- Explain that this tool is for pediatric symptom triage only
- Redirect to appropriate resources:
  - Adult symptoms: GP or adult emergency services
  - Non-health queries: Mumzworld customer service
  - Mental health: local mental health crisis services
  - Dental: dentist or dental emergency services

IMPORTANT: Never attempt to triage adult symptoms using pediatric guidelines. The physiology, normal values, and management differ significantly.
